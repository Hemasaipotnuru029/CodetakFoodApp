ABOUT FOOD_APP


FOOD_APP is a prominent online food ordering and delivery platform based in India.

Founded in 2014 by Sriharsha Majety, Nandan Reddy, and Rahul Jaimini, FOOD_APP has quickly grown to become one of the

leading players in the Indian food delivery market.FOOD_APP's journey reflects not only its growth and evolution as a business

but also its impact on the broader ecosystem of food delivery and digital commerce in India and potentially beyond.


Services and Operations:

    Delivery: Offers diverse cuisine options from local eateries to high-end restaurants.

    Foodapp Genie: Multi-purpose delivery for packages, groceries, and more.

    Foodapp Instamart: Rapid grocery and essentials delivery.

    Foodapp One: Subscription service with free deliveries and discounts.

    Foodapp Access: Cloud kitchens for restaurant expansion.

    POP and Daily: Affordable single-serve meals and subscription-based home-style meals.
